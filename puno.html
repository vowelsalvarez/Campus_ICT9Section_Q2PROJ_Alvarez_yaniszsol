Puno's Ice Cream & Sherbet

Ingredients:

- Ice cream typically contains the following ingredients: Milk or cream A sweetener, like sugar or an alternative A spice, like vanilla or cocoa Fruit, like strawberries or peaches Food coloring Stabilizers

Procedure:

- Procedure

The general steps for making ice cream are:

Blend the ingredients together

Pasteurize the mixture

Homogenize the mixture to create a consistent texture

Age the mixture

Add liquid flavors and colors

Freeze the mixture

Add fruits, nuts, and bulky flavorings

Package the ice cream
