Buko Lumpia

Ingredients:

ngredients: For the Filling:

2 cups of fresh buko (young coconut) strips or shredded buko (you can also use canned if fresh isnâ€™t available)

1/2 cup of sweetened condensed milk

1/4 cup of sugar (adjust based on sweetness preference)

1/4 cup of crushed or chopped sweetened jackfruit (optional, for added flavor)

1/4 cup of grated cheese (optional, for a creamy twist)

1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional, for extra flavor)

For the Lumpia Wrapper:

10 to 12 lumpia wrappers (spring roll wrappers, available at most Asian markets) For Frying:

Cooking oil (enough to deep fry) For Serving:

Sugar (for dusting the fried lumpia, optional)

Procedure:

- Procedure:

Prepare the Filling:

In a bowl, combine the fresh buko (coconut strips or shredded coconut),

sweetened condensed milk, sugar, jackfruit (if using), and grated cheese (if using).

Mix everything well until the ingredients are evenly incorporated.

Lay a lumpia wrapper flat on a clean surface with one corner facing you (like a diamond shape).

Place about 2-3 tablespoons of the coconut mixture near the bottom corner of the wrapper.

Fold the bottom corner over the filling, then fold in the two side corners to secure the filling inside.

Roll the wrapper tightly, ensuring the filling is sealed inside. Use a little bit of water to seal the top edge of the wrapper to prevent it from unrolling.

Fry the Lumpia: Heat oil in a deep pan or wok over medium heat (enough to submerge the lumpia).
