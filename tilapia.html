tilapia ice cream.

Ingredients:

- Ingredients:

For the Tilapia Ice Cream Base:

2 tilapia fillets (about 200-250g)

1 cup of heavy cream

1/2 cup of whole milk

1/4 cup of sugar (you can adjust to your taste, though ice cream tends to have some sweetness)

1 tablespoon of lemon juice or zest (to enhance flavor)

1/4 teaspoon of salt (adjust for seasoning)

1/4 teaspoon of white pepper (optional, for a bit of spice)

1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional, but it can complement the flavor)

Procedure:

- Procedure Prepare the Tilapia:

Season the tilapia fillets lightly with salt and pepper.

Heat olive oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.

Cook the tilapia fillets until they are fully cooked, about 4-5 minutes on each side, depending on thickness.

The fish should be flaky and white.

Once cooked, remove the tilapia from the pan and let it cool.

After it has cooled, flake the fish into small pieces, removing any bones.
