Pancit

Ingredients:

ingredients: Ingredients for Pork Sisig: Main Ingredients:

1 lb (450g) pork belly, boiled until tender (you can also use parts like pig’s ears, cheek, and snout)

1/2 lb (225g) pork liver (optional, but adds flavor)

1 onion, finely chopped

2-3 cloves garlic, minced

2-3 long green chilies (siling pang-sigang), chopped (adjust to spice preference)

1 tablespoon soy sauce

2 tablespoons calamansi juice (or lemon juice if unavailable)

1 tablespoon vinegar (preferably cane vinegar)

1/2 teaspoon salt (to taste)

1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper (to taste)

1/4 cup mayonnaise (optional, for a creamier sisig)

1-2 tablespoons cooking oil (for frying)

1 egg (optional, for serving)

1 tablespoon butter (for frying, optional)

Procedure:

- Procedure: Prepare the Ingredients:

In another pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Sauté the chopped garlic and onion until softened and fragrant. Add the chopped chilies and cook for another 1-2 minutes. Combine the Meat and Seasoning:

Add the fried pork belly and liver to the pan with the garlic, onion, and chilies. Stir well to combine. Pour in the soy sauce, vinegar, and calamansi juice. Season with salt and pepper. Cook for 3-5 minutes to let the flavors meld together. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Optional: Add Mayonnaise and Butter:

For a creamy version, add mayonnaise and stir it into the mixture. You can also add a tablespoon of butter for richness. Continue to cook for another 1-2 minutes until everything is well-combined and heated through. Serve Sizzling (Optional):

To serve the sisig the traditional way, heat a sizzling plate or cast-iron skillet. Once hot, transfer the sisig mixture onto the plate. If desired, crack a raw egg on top of the sisig and let the heat from the sizzling plate cook it slightly, mixing it into the dish for extra richness. Garnish and Serve:

Garnish with chopped green onions and serve with calamansi or lemon wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrusy flavor.
