Pancit

Ingredients:

ngredients: Noodles:

1 lb (450g) pancit canton (egg noodles) â€“ available in Filipino or Asian grocery stores Meats and Seafood:

1/2 lb (225g) chicken breast or thigh (boneless, skinless), cut into small strips

1/2 lb (225g) shrimp, peeled and deveined

1/2 lb (225g) pork (preferably pork belly or pork shoulder), sliced thinly

Optional: 2-3 Chinese sausage links (lapa) or hotdog (sliced)

Vegetables:

1 medium onion, finely chopped

2-3 cloves garlic, minced

1 medium carrot, julienned

1/2 cup green beans, sliced diagonally

1 cup cabbage, shredded

1 red bell pepper, julienned (optional)

Seasonings and Sauces:

2 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp oyster sauce

1 tbsp fish sauce (optional)

Salt and pepper to taste

1/2 cup chicken broth or water

1-2 tbsp vegetable oil (for stir-frying)

Garnish:

Lemon or calamansi wedges

Chopped green onions or fried garlic (optional)

Procedure:

- Procedure: Prepare the Ingredients:

Cook the pancit canton noodles according to package instructions.

Drain and set aside. If the noodles seem too dry after draining, you can toss them with a little bit of oil to prevent sticking.

Slice the chicken, pork, and shrimp into small pieces, and prepare the vegetables by chopping the onion, garlic, carrots, and bell pepper.

Cook the Meats and Seafood:

Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.

Add the pork and cook until it turns brown and crispy (about 4-5 minutes). Remove and set aside.

In the same pan, add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through (about 3-4 minutes). Remove and set aside.

Add the shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes. Remove and set aside.
