Boodle fight

Ingredients:

- Grilled meats, seafood, and vegetables are ideal because they retain their flavor and texture. Other common ingredients include rice, salted eggs, tomatoes, okra, bok choy, cucumbers, fresh mango, pineapple, watermelon, flan, kakanin, and maja blanca.

Procedure:

- Procedure

For salted duck eggs,

boil them in simmering water for 12 minutes,

then drain and rinse in cold water until cool.

Peel and dice,

and toss with the other salad ingredients before serving.

Place scoops of rice,

piles of pickles,

sliced vegetables or fruits,

and other shared dishes in the middle of the table.

Leave space around the edges for each guest to use as a place mat.

Layer in lettuce leaves, coconut cups,

or crisped Vietnamese sesame crackers to contain sauces and individual servings.
