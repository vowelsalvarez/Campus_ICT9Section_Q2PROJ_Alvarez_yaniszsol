Recipes

Desserts

Desserts are sweet foods typically served at the end of a meal. They are often enjoyed as a treat or indulgence and can be made from a variety of ingredients such as sugar, flour, fruits, dairy products, chocolate, and nuts.

Dishes

Dishes refer to prepared foods or meals that are typically served as part of a larger meal. A dish can be made up of one or more ingredients, and it can be a main course, a side, or a special part of the meal.

Snacks

Snacks are small portions of food or drink typically consumed between main meals to satisfy hunger, provide energy, or simply for enjoyment.

Boodle Fight
